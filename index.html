Other Projects

FrameFish

FrameFish is an eyewear virtual try-on system for ecommerce websites. At the time of development it was much faster to load and respond than competing systems, which took around 10 seconds to generate a virtual-try-on image (FrameFish takes about 1 second). FrameFish received an Innovate NSW grant and has been featured on Sky Business News and in the UNSW Young Entrepreneurs series.
